ASE Portfolio

Privacy Policy — Last updated: May 5, 2026

Overview

ASE Portfolio is a personal stock portfolio tracking application developed by Dimitris.

✓ Short version: ASE Portfolio does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers. Everything stays on your device.

Data Storage

All data is stored exclusively on your device using Room Database (SQLite), EncryptedSharedPreferences (AES-256) for API keys, and SharedPreferences for settings. None of this data leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

API Keys

If you enter Anthropic or TwelveData API keys, they are stored encrypted on your device and sent directly to those services. We never receive or have access to your API keys.

Third-Party Services

The app connects to: Yahoo Finance, TwelveData, Athens Stock Exchange (ATHEX), and Anthropic (AI chat). These services may see your device IP address as part of normal internet communication.

What We Do NOT Collect

  • Personal identification information
  • Financial portfolio data
  • Usage analytics or crash reports
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Location data

Notifications

Price alert notifications are generated entirely on your device with no external server communication from our side.

Financial Disclaimer

⚠️ Not financial advice: ASE Portfolio is a portfolio tracking tool only. Nothing in this app constitutes investment advice. Always consult a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

Children's Privacy

ASE Portfolio is not directed at children under 13 years of age.

Your Rights

  • Access: All your data is visible within the app
  • Export: Use the Excel export feature
  • Delete: Uninstall the app to permanently delete all data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
